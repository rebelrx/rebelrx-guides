# 🍎 Produce

## 🌿 High Priority Organic

Focus organic here (thin skin / higher pesticide exposure):

- Berries  
- Leafy greens  
- Apples  
- Grapes  
- Peppers  

---

## 🥑 Lower Priority Organic

If organic is out of your budget, these are generally lower priority (thick peel / lower absorption):

- Avocados  
- Onions  
- Bananas  
- Pineapple  

💡 Produce Tip:

- Follow the [EWG “Dirty Dozen / Clean Fifteen” Shopper's Guide](https://www.ewg.org/foodnews/full-list.php)

👉 Prioritize organic where pesticide exposure is highest.

---

## 🥬 Produce Best Practices

- Wash all produce
- Peel when appropriate  
- Buy seasonal when possible  

💡 Produce Washing Tip:

- Follow the [EWG's Guide to Washing Produce](https://static.ewg.org/upload/pdf/EWG_FruitVegWashing_Tipsheet.pdf)
