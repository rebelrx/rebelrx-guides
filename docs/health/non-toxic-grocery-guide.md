# 🛒 Non-Toxic Grocery Guide

Modern food systems are not optimized for human health. They are optimized for:

- Shelf life  
- Cost  
- Convenience  

!!! info "What This Guide Is"
    A practical, real-world framework focused on:

    - Whole foods  
    - Ingredient quality  
    - Minimizing toxic exposure  

---

## 👤 Who This Is For

!!! tip "This guide is for you if..."
    - You want to reduce toxic exposure  
    - You want better food quality without overcomplication  
    - You want practical grocery decisions  

👉 This is not about perfection. It’s about better defaults.

---

## ⚡ Quick Start

!!! success "If You Do Nothing Else"
    - Buy whole foods (avoid ultra-processed)
    - Prioritize pasture-raised eggs and quality meat
    - Replace seed oils with olive oil, butter, or ghee
    - Buy organic for high-residue produce
    - Store and heat food in glass (not plastic)
    - Use non-toxic cookware

---

## 🧠 Core Philosophy

!!! quote
    The fewer ingredients, the better.

If a product requires a paragraph to describe it, it’s probably not food.

---

## ☣️ Toxin Exposure Hierarchy

!!! warning "Focus here first"
    1. Ultra-processed foods  
    2. Industrial seed oils  
    3. Pesticide-heavy produce  
    4. Plastic exposure  
    5. Additives / preservatives

## 🧺 Where to Buy

- Local markets  
- Farmers markets  

!!! tip "No local options?"
    - Use direct-to-consumer brands  
    - Prioritize transparency  
    - Ask: *Can I trace this source?*

!!! warning "Large Chain Stores"
    Use selectively:

    - Complex supply chains  
    - Lower sourcing transparency  

---

## 🛒 How to Shop

=== "1️⃣ Perimeter"
    - Produce  
    - Meat / fish  
    - Dairy  

    👉 Most real food lives here  

=== "2️⃣ Ingredients"
    Ignore:
    - “Natural”
    - “Healthy”

    Focus on:
    - Short ingredient lists  
    - Recognizable foods  

=== "3️⃣ Priorities"
    If budget is limited:

    - Quality protein  
    - High-residue produce  
    - Dairy (if consumed)

---

## 🥩 Protein Sources

### 🥚 Eggs

=== "Ranking"
    1. Pasture-raised  
    2. Organic  
    3. Cage-free  
    4. Conventional  

=== "Label Reality"
    !!! warning
        - “Natural” → meaningless  
        - “No hormones” → irrelevant  
        - “Vegetarian-fed” → misleading  

---

### 🐄 Meat

=== "Quality"
    - Grass-fed & finished → best  
    - Grass-fed → acceptable  
    - Conventional → lowest  

---

### 🐟 Fish

!!! success "Best"
    - Wild-caught  

!!! warning "Limit Mercury"
    - Tuna  
    - Swordfish  

!!! tip "Better Options"
    - Salmon  
    - Sardines  
    - Anchovies

## 🧈 Fats & Oils

=== "✅ Use"
    - Butter / ghee  
    - Beef tallow  
    - Olive oil  
    - Avocado oil  

=== "⚠️ Use Carefully"
    - Coconut oil  
    - Flaxseed oil (cold only)  
    - Sesame oil (low heat)  

=== "🚫 Avoid"
    - Canola  
    - Soybean  
    - Corn oil  
    - Margarine  

---

### 🫒 Olive Oil

!!! warning "Fraud is common"
    Look for:
    - Harvest date  
    - Traceability  
    - Third-party certifications  

!!! tip "Best strategy"
    Choose transparent, traceable brands

---
